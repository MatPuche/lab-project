Validation

In this section, we compared the two maps in terms of their accuracy. To do so, the maps were reclassified to obtain thematic maps of built/not built areas (1 denotes not built and 2 denotes built).

Then, it was created a map of differences to visualize where the maps have the same classification and where they are different. The areas are classified as: 0 (GHS not populated and WorldPop populated), 1 (GHS and WolrdPop either both populated or unpopulated) and 2 (GHS populated and WorldPop not populated)

Using the map of differences, sampling points were selected and using photo-interpretation of satellite images, we classified the points manually to do the comparison.
